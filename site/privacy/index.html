privacy policy

nox · last updated June 21, 2026

nox collects nothing. It has no account, no server, no analytics, no advertising, no tracking, and no third-party code. It makes no network connections. Everything nox does happens entirely on your device, and nothing ever leaves it.

information we collect

None. nox does not collect, transmit, sell, share, or have access to any personal data, usage data, or device identifiers. There is no account to create and no information to provide.

information stored on your device

So the app can work, nox stores the following locally on your device only (in the app's private storage):

  • the websites you choose to block;
  • your turn-off delay and whether blocking is currently on;
  • your app/category selection, which is provided by Apple's Screen Time picker as opaque tokens — nox cannot read which apps you chose, only act on them.

This information never leaves your device, is never sent to us or anyone else, and is not accessible to us. We never see it.

screen time (family controls)

nox uses Apple's Family Controls / Screen Time framework, with your explicit permission, solely to apply the app and website restrictions you configure. This processing happens on-device under Apple's system. nox does not receive, store, or transmit any data through this framework, and the permission is used only to enforce the limits you set for yourself.

network & third parties

nox does not connect to the internet, contains no third-party SDKs, no analytics or advertising frameworks, and shares data with no one. There are no third parties involved because no data exists to share.

children's privacy

nox does not knowingly collect personal information from anyone, including children under 13. Because nox collects no data from any user, it collects none from children.

data retention & deletion

The on-device data described above persists only while nox is installed. Deleting nox removes all of its data from your device. There is nothing for us to retain or delete because we never hold any of it.

changes to this policy

If this policy changes, the updated version will be posted on this page with a new "last updated" date.
